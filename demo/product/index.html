NUTMEG FLAVOUR

This variant is a tribute to the spice-rich groves of Wayanad and Tamil Nadu, where nutmeg has been cultivated for centuries. Its distinct aroma brings warmth to every sip, ideal in spiced cocktails.

  • Tasting Notes: Fragrant, peppery warmth, smooth finish
  • Perfect for spiced meats, warm cocktails
  • Inspired by spice plantations and herbal brews of South India

GOOSEBERRY FLAVOUR

The original Toddy flavour brings a fresh and tangy burst of gooseberry (amla) to the drink, a fruit revered in Ayurveda. This refreshing blend is perfect for light snacks and summer evenings.

  • Tasting Notes: Tangy, citrusy freshness, subtle herbal undertones
  • Pairs great with coastal cuisine or light snacks
  • Inspired by traditional toddy shop recipes from Kerala

CLASSIC RED

Classic Red is the signature blend of Ottakomban — bold, full-bodied and spicy. Inspired by the toddy tapping traditions of South India, it offers warm spice and a woody finish.

  • Tasting Notes: Warm spices, slight smokiness, woody mouthfeel
  • Enjoy neat or with soda
  • Inspired by South India’s toddy legacy
